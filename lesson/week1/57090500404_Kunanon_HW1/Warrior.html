Warriors are a part of the Explorers. They deliver attacks with brutal efficiency and raw strength. Warriors have the highest base HP and the lowest base MP of any class. A Warrior can wield the widest variety of weapons in the game, from swords and axes to spears and blunt weapons, and they can also use shields. The Warrior class branches off into three directions depending on what weapon they choose to specialize in.
