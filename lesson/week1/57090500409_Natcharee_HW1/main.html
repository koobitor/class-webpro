    Oh My Girl


    Oh My Girl is an eight-member South Korean girl group formed by WM Entertainment. They debuted on April 21, 2015. Oh My Girl was publicized as the new sister group of label-mate group B1A4. In March 29, 2015, teaser images of the eight members were released as well as their concept images. On April 20, the group released their first mini album, Oh My Girl, which included the single "Cupid". They held their debut showcase the same day. They first performed the single on SBS MTV's The Show.
